London is a Canadian city in southwestern Ontario, located just north of Lake Erie and the U.S.
border. It's home to the University of Western Ontario. Among the city's range of museums are Museum London, a showcase for regional art and historical artifacts, and the interactive Children's Museum.
The city centre features numerous parks and greenways along the Thames River.
